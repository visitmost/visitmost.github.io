If I don’t use Windows or Mac, can I still do these things:

– create documents, spreadsheets and presentations
– edit and organise photos from my camera/phone
– design 3D models and animations
– play games
– video or text chat
– use internet banking
– download and enjoy music and movies

The great answer is YES!

Linux is the great, free desktop operating system which is super capable and fun to tinker with. In the spirit of number 8 wire, come and learn how to get started.

We’ll cover the basics of finding or repurposing low cost computers, installing one of the many modern, free Linux operating systems and handling the software that allows you to do popular activities.
